# 🎤 Voice Input Quick Guide

## How to Use Voice Input

### Step 1: Click Microphone Button
- Look for the microphone icon 🎤 on your calculator
- Click it to activate voice recognition

### Step 2: Allow Microphone Access
- Browser will ask for permission
- Click "Allow" to grant microphone access

### Step 3: Speak Your Math Problem
**Examples:**
- "Five plus three"
- "Ten divided by two"
- "Seven times eight"
- "Twenty minus fifteen"

### Step 4: Watch It Calculate
- Your speech appears as math expression
- Calculator automatically solves it
- Voice speaks the answer!

---

## 🔊 Voice Output Examples

After calculation, you'll hear:
- "The answer is 8"
- "The answer is 5"
- "The answer is 56"

---

## 📋 Supported Commands

### Basic Operations:
| Say This | Becomes |
|----------|---------|
| "plus" | + |
| "minus" | - |
| "times" or"multiply" | * |
| "divided by" | / |

### Numbers:
Just say the numbers naturally:
- "Five" → 5
- "Twenty-three" → 23
- "One hundred" → 100

### Advanced:
| Say This | Becomes |
|----------|---------|
| "to the power of" | ** |
| "square root" | sqrt|
| "point" (for decimals) | . |

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Browser Requirements:
✅ Works best on: Chrome, Edge  
⚠️ Limited support: Firefox  
❌ Not supported: Internet Explorer  

### Microphone Works Only On:
- **HTTPS websites** OR
- **Localhost** (http://localhost:5000) ✅

### Tips for Best Results:
1. Speak clearly and at normal pace
2. Avoid background noise
3. Make sure microphone isn't muted
4. Wait for red pulse animation before speaking

---

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "Microphone not working"
1. Check browser permissions
2. Go to: Settings → Privacy → Microphone → Allow
3. Refresh page and try again

### "Voice not recognized"
1. Speak louder/clearer
2. Check microphone connection
3. Try: "five plus three" (simple test)

### "No voice output"
1. Check system volume
2. Verify speakers working
3. Check Flask terminal for errors
